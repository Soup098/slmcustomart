How To Order

Follow the path below. Each step moves your personalized portrait a little closer to the wall it will hang on.

1

Let's talk

Contact me via email at slmcustomart@gmail.com. I’ll get in touch with you to discuss your needs and answer any questions you may have.

Contact Me!
2

Provide visuals

Send a few photos by email, along with a 50% deposit. Don’t forget to check out our photo tips for the best results.

Photo Tips
3

Quality control

After receiving your photos and deposit, I’ll send an original sketch photo for your input. Then you’ll receive a photo of the finished watercolor portrait for your approval. Once you give the green light and make the final 50% payment, I will mail the painting to you.

4

The nitty gritty

Timing depends on the complexity of the portrait and order volume at the time, but you can usually expect your painting finished in 2–3 weeks. Shipping to the continental United States is included in the price, though delivery times vary by location — so please keep that in mind when ordering.

*Payments can be made by Cash (if local), check, or Venmo.
