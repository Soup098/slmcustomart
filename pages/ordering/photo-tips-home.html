Follow these tips when photographing your property.

Great photos make for a great portrait. A few simple things will help me capture your home exactly the way you love it.

Tip #1:

Send clear photos of your property from the angle (or angles) you like — multiple angles are always best.

Tip #2:

Take some close-ups of any details you’d like included.

Tip #3:

The house or structure should be the main focus. Place the garage and/or driveway toward the far side of the frame.

Tip #4:

Photograph your home in different light — at dawn, at dusk, or on a cloudy day. Colors are truest when there’s no harsh midday sun.

Tip #5:

I can remove any objects you don’t want in the portrait. Learn more about your personalization options.
